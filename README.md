## SB Comfort Detailing

SB Comfort Detailing is a local business in my hometown that details your cars, trucks, camper vans, and more. What makes them special is that they do this all from the comfort of your own home! 

The situation they were in was that they were wanting to bring this business to life, and create an online presence so that they could show off their work and company along with guiding their customers to book appointments.

I created a website that allowed for all this to happen and more! The outline of this website is as follows:

### Home Page (index.html)

The home page features a large graphic that grabs the user's attention. It's catch phrase ("Auto and Marine Detailing from the Comfort of Your Own Home!") is desirable for it's appropriate audience. 

As you scroll down, you see an advertisement for a specific promotion going on at the company. Below that, you see a description of the company along with two pictures of the company's founders doing detailing work on some of their vehicles. 

### About Page (about.html)

The about page features descriptions of all members of the company. Below that, a description of the company origin story and a collage of photos of their completed detail projects. 

### Order Page (order.html)

This page has 6 buttons, each of them leading to a different google form for a different vehicle. Below these, you see a list of discount packages.
